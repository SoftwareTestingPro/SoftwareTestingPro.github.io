🍪 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They help us remember your preferences and improve your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Analytics Cookies: Track site usage via Google Analytics
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense and affiliate networks
  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for extended periods

Opt-Out & Control

You can control cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being set
  • Google Analytics Opt-Out: Download Google Analytics Opt-Out Extension
  • Google Ads Settings: Manage Your Google Ads Preferences
  • GDPR Consent: Users in EU/EEA may see a consent banner

Note: Disabling cookies may affect some website features.

👤 Your Rights & Choices

GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)

If you're in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of certain data processing activities
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

CCPA Rights (California Residents)

If you're a California resident, you have the following rights under CCPA:

  • Right to Know: Request what personal data we collect and how it's used
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of the "sale or sharing" of personal information
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@softwaretestingpro.github.io
  • GitHub Issues: Report on GitHub

Response Time: We aim to respond to all requests within 30 days. We may request additional information to verify your identity.
