About This Website

This platform is a comprehensive, open-source software automation practice tool designed specifically for Selenium beginners. It provides a wide range of tasks aimed at those who struggle to find real-world scenarios to automate. By working through these tasks, users can deepen their understanding of Selenium concepts and improve their coding skills. The platform allows you to explore what works and what doesn’t when writing automation scripts, helping you overcome common challenges. It’s a valuable resource for mastering Selenium, offering practical experience and boosting confidence in solving automation problems independently.
