📌 Overview

Affiliate relationships do not influence our recommendations or the quality of our content. You pay the same price whether you click through our affiliate links or visit the merchant directly.

🔗 Affiliate Links & Programs

What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special URLs that track when you click from our site to a merchant's website. If you make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. This is a standard way educational and informational websites sustain their operations.

How to Identify Affiliate Links

We disclose affiliate links clearly by:

  • Adding "[AD]" or "[Affiliate Link]" tags near or before the link
  • Including a note in the surrounding text about the affiliate relationship
  • Using distinct styling or visual indicators when applicable
  • Always stating: "This is an affiliate link" or similar language
Your Right to Know: We believe you have the right to know when a link is an affiliate link. If you ever see a link that you believe should be disclosed and isn't, please contact us immediately.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do affiliate links cost me more?

No. When you use our affiliate links, you pay the exact same price. The merchant compensates us directly out of their marketing budget.

Why do you use affiliate links?

Maintaining a free learning platform requires resources. Affiliate commissions help us cover hosting, development, and content creation costs while keeping the platform free for everyone.

Can I avoid affiliate links?

Yes. If you prefer to avoid affiliate links, you can:

  • Search for products directly in your browser
  • Use Google or another search engine
  • Visit merchant websites directly

How do I know if a link is an affiliate link?

Look for disclosure language like "[AD]," "[Affiliate Link]," or "This link is an affiliate link." If you're unsure, hover over the link or contact us.

Are your reviews biased?

No. We maintain strict editorial standards. Our reviews are based on quality, value, and community feedback—not commission potential. We recommend products we genuinely believe in.

Do you sell my data?

No. We do not sell your personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details on how we handle information.
