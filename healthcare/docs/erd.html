The core schema of Shukla Healthcare Systems. Use this to understand the links between Employees, Claims, and Providers.

Business Flow

1. Onboarding: Employers sign up with insurance products.
2. Enrollment: Employees choose which products to subscribe to.
3. Claims: When a service is performed, a claim is generated against an active enrollment.
